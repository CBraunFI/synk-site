About us

Who we are

For over 25 years, we have helped people, teams and organizations unlock their potential.

„We ignite human magic to make every company future proof.“Our purpose

We work for and with people. Their ability to change themselves and the world is unique. We are convinced that everyone has individual strengths and talents – and we want to be a reliable companion on the path to excellence.

How we work – four levels

Purpose

„We ignite human magic to make every company future proof.“ Our guiding principle: we spark what makes people effective – so that companies stay fit for the future.

Approach

„Everyone wants to do something meaningful. To put their strengths and talents to use. To be recognized and to make a real difference.“ Our approach starts with the individual and scales to the team and organizational level.

Method

„We think hybrid. We work on site and digitally, and connect these two worlds to deliver the greatest value.“ This is how we create interactive knowledge transfer across a wide range of formats.

Community

For more than 25 years we have guided companies through leadership, collaboration and change processes – carried by an established network. Formats such as the SYNK Collaboration Labs, Leadership Briefings and the Young Women Leadership Program keep it alive.

Methods & tools

There are no limits to creativity in knowledge transfer. Depending on the goal and context, we combine:

  • Seminar actors
  • Role plays
  • Peer case consulting
  • Leada app
  • E-learnings (incl. AI fundamentals)
  • Virtual Reality (VR)
  • Switched Classroom
