Project report · Bayer AG

Lessons Learned – Knowledge Management

Facilitation, preparation and documentation of a standardised lessons-learned process from completed CapEx projects – with more than 150 lessons-learned one-pagers for the Bayer knowledge database.

‹ All projects

The situation

To systematically harness the experience from completed CapEx projects, Bayer AG established a standardised lessons-learned process – with a knowledge database, a knowledge-uptake workshop and one to two mandatory lessons-learned workshops. Bayer asked SYNK to support the facilitation of the workshops within this process.

Our approach

For each lessons-learned workshop, SYNK analyses a survey among the project participants, prepares the workshop based on the results and facilitates it. The results are documented in a Bayer standard template and translated into English to be stored in the Bayer knowledge database.

Content & services

  • Analysis of the survey results per project
  • Medium-sized on-site lessons-learned workshops and/or online workshops in small groups
  • Facilitation, documentation, project management and translation
  • Further development of the lessons-learned process
