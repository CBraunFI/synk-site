Project report · Deutsche Bahn / DB Akademie

New as a middle manager

A blended-learning programme that strengthens Deutsche Bahn’s new middle managers in their role – since 2016, more than 2,450 participants have realised their own transition initiative.

‹ All projects

The situation

Deutsche Bahn’s middle managers who are new to this role receive practical and effective support to successfully manage expectations, opportunities and challenges within their new area of responsibility. The complexity of the new leadership task is made understandable and manageable.

Our approach

The programme follows a blended-learning approach: it begins with intensive preparation – stakeholder interviews, SWOT analysis and web-based learning. This is followed by two two-day in-person modules with digital transfer support, an open-space compact workshop, vision work and experience-based practical exercises. The whole is complemented by an individual fit analysis, one-to-one coaching and a large-group event to celebrate what has been achieved and to strengthen the culture of responsibility.

Content & services

Participants experience an inspiring development journey, learn to manage the demands of their new role more easily and realise an individual, self-chosen transition initiative – strengthening entrepreneurial creative and change competence and contributing directly to the company’s performance improvement.
