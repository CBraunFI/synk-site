Project report · Lufthansa Group

Leadership Starter Programme

A hybrid programme for developing emerging leaders – co-creatively developed and rolled out worldwide, for a unified leadership mindset across the Lufthansa Group.

‹ All projects

The situation

In the face of rapidly changing conditions, the Lufthansa Group wants to transform its leadership culture in order to remain successful in the long term. A key lever in this: the contemporary qualification and development of emerging leaders towards a leadership mindset that is consistent across the LH Group.

Our approach

Based on the LHG-specific needs, a hybrid programme was developed in a co-creative process with various stakeholders. The design and worldwide delivery are continuously adapted – a short-notice switch between in-person and online formats is possible at any time.

Content & services

The programme currently comprises five modules:

  • Leading at Lufthansa Group in the 21st century
  • Inspiring Leadership
  • Driving Performance
  • Communication / Team Development & Collaboration
  • Hybrid Leadership
  • Leading Agile & Keep Growing
