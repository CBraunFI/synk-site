E-Learning: AI Fundamentals

The fundamentals of working with artificial intelligence – multimedia and non-linear.

In clear, multimedia courses, SYNK experts bring you up to speed on working with AI. You decide how much time you invest and which areas to focus on.

Programme & details

At a glance

Target group

Teams and employees in companies – the interactive e-learning for focused team onboarding on the topic of AI.

Duration

Completion time approx. 45–60 minutes.

Format

Interactive online self-study e-learning (Articulate Storyline, HTML5). Delivered as a SCORM or xAPI package for common LMS platforms or as a standalone website.

Certification

Personalised certificate to download at the end. Multiple-choice knowledge checks after each section. Compliance reference to the EU AI Act and GDPR.

Investment

On request – a B2B module, individually adaptable to your company.

Structure

Six chapters with interactive navigation, table of contents, glossary and full-screen mode.

Content & modules

  • Introduction and fundamentals: what AI is and what it is not – forms of learning and limits
  • Of humans and machines: how AI systems come about (the OMA principle)
  • Understanding data: big data, correlation vs. causation, bias & fairness
  • Language models and LLMs: text generation and prompt engineering
  • Risks of AI: responsible use, checks & balances
  • Legal foundations: the EU AI Act with its risk classes and GDPR references
