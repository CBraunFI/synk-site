Qualification

European Business Coach

A sound, recognised coaching qualification for the lasting support of people and organisations.

You learn to support people and teams professionally – with a sound methodological repertoire, plenty of practice and qualified feedback. The training combines attitude, tools and self-experience.

Dates & registration

At a glance

Target group

Leaders, HR professionals, consultants and self-employed individuals who want to support, empower and develop others on a sound and lasting basis.

Duration

Six months of intensive training with three weeks in person and 322 hours of coaching practice.

Format

Hybrid – in-person and digital units, supported by Leada and the SYNLIFE learning platform (video analysis, mindmaps, interactive exercises).

Certification

Certified according to DIN PAS 1029 (individual coaching) and ICF-ACSTH; audited by the Forschungsstelle Coaching-Gutachten. Over 1,000 graduates in the alumni network.

Investment

8,465.00 € (exempt from VAT).

Dates 2026

Week 1: 08–12.06. · Week 2: 14–18.09. · Week 3: 23–27.11.2026. Current dates and further course starts on request.

Content & modules

  • Fundamentals of business coaching: relationship quality, rapport and the quality triangle according to PAS 1029
  • Developing the coaching process: questioning and communication techniques, solution-oriented brief consulting
  • Systemic self-management
  • Systemic business coaching
  • Analysis of perspectives, personality and motivation
  • Resource-oriented business coaching
  • Dealing with attitudes, mindsets and conflicts
  • Culture, identity and values
  • Leadership and change management: leadership models, the RAFAEL method, organisational development
  • Coaching groups, examination and final colloquium
