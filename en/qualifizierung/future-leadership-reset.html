Future Leadership Reset

Realign your leadership – a programme for the demands of tomorrow.

The Future Leadership Reset invites specialists and leaders in transition to consciously rethink their role – between attitude, effectiveness and the demands of a dynamic working world.

Dates & registration

At a glance

Target group

Specialists and leaders in transition who want to reorient themselves professionally or bring their leadership qualities to a future-ready level.

Duration

Multi-month online programme, part-time and at your own pace.

Format

100 % digital training – fully online and asynchronous via the Leada app, complemented by webinars, self-reflections and case studies.

Certification

Recognised certificate of participation upon completion.

Investment

On request – up to 100 % eligible for funding.

Focus

Self-leadership, change, digital competence and future vision – closely interwoven with your own leadership routine in a practical way.

Content & modules

  • Personal development & self-leadership
  • Communication & conflict management
  • Change management & agility
  • Digital competences & AI
  • Leadership & teamwork
  • Sustainability & diversity
  • Future vision & career planning

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who is the programme for?

For specialists and leaders in professional transition or reorientation.

Yes, depending on the prerequisites (e.g. via the Federal Employment Agency). We advise you on suitable funding.

Is everything online?

Yes, 100% online and therefore location-independent.
