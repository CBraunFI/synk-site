Qualification

StärkenCoach

Developing people through their strengths – the heart of the SYNK philosophy as a qualification.

Learn to make strengths and talents visible and to develop them in a targeted way. Instead of working on weaknesses, you unlock potential – in yourself and in the people you support.

Dates & enrolment

At a glance

Target group

Coaches and leaders with an existing basic coaching qualification (e.g. European Business Coach) who want to work in a strengths-based way.

Duration

Four days in total (2 × 2 days across two module weeks).

Format

In person in Frankfurt. Working with SYNK GROUP’s systemic strengths approach, CliftonStrengths 34 and the 24 character strengths according to Seligman/Peterson.

Certification

Certificate as a SYNK StrengthsCoach upon successfully passing the examination.

Investment

3,200.00 €.

Focus

Positive psychology, strengths and brain research – methodologically sound and directly transferable into coaching practice.

Dates 2026

Module 1: 19–20.03. · Module 2: 14–15.05.2026 (Frankfurt). Another cohort in autumn – current dates and availability on request.

Funding

Depending on your federal state and status, funding via an education voucher, the Qualifizierungschancengesetz or educational leave may be available – just ask.

Content & modules

  • Getting to know the strengths approach: SYNK GROUP’s systemic strengths approach, strengths and brain research
  • Understanding your own strengths: CliftonStrengths with 34 talent themes and talent combinations
  • Putting it into coaching practice
  • Reflecting on both strengths approaches: the 24 character strengths according to Seligman/Peterson
  • Questioning techniques in strengths coaching
  • Strengths-coaching conversation methods and techniques

„Only those who discover and develop their strengths and virtues can unfold their full potential. And anyone who is also able to develop the strengths and virtues of others can model genuine, lasting leadership.“– Christine Ait-Mokhtar, SYNK GROUP
