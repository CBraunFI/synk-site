Qualification

Systemischer Business Coach (Online)

Systemic coaching competence, fully online and alongside your job.

In this online programme you acquire systemic coaching competence – flexible, work-compatible and hands-on. Ideal for anyone who wants to learn independently of location and apply it straight away.

Dates & enrolment

At a glance

Target group

People who, as a Systemic Business Coach, want to analyse social systems, understand interactions and develop paths to a solution.

Duration

Six months of distance learning with 80 hours of coaching practice.

Format

Online distance learning, flexible in time and place. Learning with the Leada App, personal mentoring and telephone coaching, seminar materials and email tutorial.

Certification

Certificate as a Systemic Business Coach upon successful completion of the programme.

Investment

2,850.00 €.

Start

Possible at any time – the date is agreed individually after registration.

Funding

Depending on your federal state and status, funding via an education voucher, the Qualifizierungschancengesetz or educational leave may be available – just ask.

Content & modules

  • Systemic coaching in the context of face-to-face relationships and intrapersonal aspects
  • Systemic coaching in the context of teams and groups
  • Systemic coaching in the context of organisations
  • Case analysis of a coaching situation (case 1)
  • Producing a written coaching documentation
  • Literature study
  • Case analysis of a coaching situation (case 2)
  • Final assignment

„As a Systemic Business Coach you develop a holistic view of complex social situations. You learn to actively perceive interactions and interdependencies and to work with them constructively.“– Dr. Jörg Krauter, SYNK GROUP
