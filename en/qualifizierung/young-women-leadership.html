Young Women Leadership Program

Our scholarship and programme for emerging female leaders.

The Young Women Leadership Program supports emerging female leaders in their development in a targeted way – with training, mentoring and a strong network.

Dates & enrolment

At a glance

Target group

Young women at the start of their leadership careers – explicitly including those in start-ups.

Duration

Six months, with two intensive weeks as the core of the leadership training.

Format

Hybrid – in-person units and online courses, complemented by senior leadership coaching with a personal mentor for each participant.

Distinction

Designed as a SYNK scholarship – awarded since 2016, around 60 graduates.

Funding

No fee payable – a scholarship worth 6,500.00 €.

Dates 2026

Module 1: 24–27 Aug · Module 2: 12–15 Oct 2026. The application process is open.

Content & modules

  • Working with data, artificial intelligence and the digitalisation of the working world
  • Tools for self-reflection and effective communication
  • Leading and developing employees
  • Developing organisations
  • Senior leadership coaching with a personal sparring partner
  • Building a cross-industry YWL network

„The Young Women Leadership Program was an extremely enriching experience. Surrounded by inspiring women, I was able to gain valuable impulses on leadership, ESG and sustainability – aspects that will have a lasting influence on my professional development.“– Programme participant
