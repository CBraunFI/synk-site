What Leada can do

Theme packages

Curated, dialogue-based learning content that unfolds over days and weeks like a conversation – pausable and with a clear completion status. From conversation techniques to strategy development.

AI impulse assistant

Proactive, situational prompts for the day or the next meeting – explicitly as a conversational impulse, not as an instruction. A gentle offer instead of pressure.

Strengths & role context

Leada knows your strengths, challenges and context. Based on your personal strengths profile, a role dialogue emerges – real ownership for topics and roles.

Training support

A short pre-work analysis before the training, goal definition during the training, after-work transfer afterwards. Regular short prompts remind you of your goals.

Team & company functions

Teams join via a company code: team chat, document upload, anonymized team aggregation and strengths matching – who brings which strengths to a specific task.

Real-time insights for HR

Content and team analytics with PDF export give leadership and HR a reliable real-time overview of the wishes and needs of the workforce – as a basis for steering decisions.

Technology & data protection – transparent

How the AI works

Leada uses AI as an impulse assistant: it generates situational conversation prompts based on your strengths profile and context – deliberately as an offer, not an automated decision.

Data basis

Fed by your own analyses and inputs. Team evaluations are anonymised and aggregated – no individual monitoring.

Data protection & hosting

GDPR-compliant, hosting in the EU. Published by Leada AG, Frankfurt am Main. We’re happy to clarify data-processing details transparently – request a demo & tech details.
